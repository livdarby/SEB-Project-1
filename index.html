How to play

Your cabbages are almost ready to harvest!

But an infestation of caterpillars threatens to destroy your cabbages before you have a chance to pick them!

Your goal is to use the numbers on the tiles to determine where the caterpillars are hiding in the cabbage patch. Right click to place a jar on each infested tile, safely catching the caterpillar. Each number tells you how many bordering tiles contain caterpillars.

If you accidentally click on a caterpillar, it will munch through your cabbages and the game will be over!

But successfully catch all the caterpillars in jars, and you win the game. Your cabbages are safe from hungry bugs and it's time to release the caterpillars into the wild!
